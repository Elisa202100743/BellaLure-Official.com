Bella Lure is a prestigious luxury brand of fine jewellery. Elisa Marielle founded the company “Bella Lure” in 2022, where she works with local jewelers that use premium and long-lasting materials, including precious metals such as recycled and ethically mined gold. Her jewelry has a strong purpose and combines dynamic movement with a strong type of elegance.

“Bella Lure” brand is a french name and it brings to mind the French expression “avoir de l’allure,” which signifies to have both elegance and style and that’s what represents my brand. And the word “Belle” which means beautiful, is to reflect our jewellery as well as femininity and the sense of beauty and elegance that Bella Lure jewellery gives ladies.
